# CPF Calculation Rules

## Rounding (sacrosanct)
1. Total CPF = round to nearest dollar (>= 0.50 rounds up, < 0.50 rounds down)
2. Employee share = always round DOWN (Math.floor)
3. Employer share = Total - Employee (derived, never independently calculated)

## Wage Ceilings (2026)
- OW Monthly: S$8,000
- Annual Salary Ceiling: S$102,000
- AW Ceiling: S$102,000 - Total OW for year
- Annual CPF Limit: S$37,740

## Age Bands (SC / PR 3rd Year+)
| Age Band | Employer | Employee | Total |
|----------|----------|----------|-------|
| 55 and below | 17% | 20% | 37% |
| Above 55 to 60 | 16% | 18% | 34% |
| Above 60 to 65 | 12.5% | 12.5% | 25% |
| Above 65 to 70 | 9% | 7.5% | 16.5% |
| Above 70 | 7.5% | 5% | 12.5% |

## Date Rules
- Age-band transition: applies from 1st of month AFTER birthday
- PR status change: applies from 1st of month AFTER status change
- Pro-ration: calendar days (days worked / total days in month)
